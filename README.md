# 📊 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔷 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end business analysis of e-commerce data using Power BI and Excel.
The goal is to analyze sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior to generate actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze revenue, profit, and growth trends
* Identify factors affecting profitability
* Understand customer behavior and segmentation
* Provide data-driven business recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
* Excel (Data Analysis & Preparation)
* Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
* DAX (Measures & Calculations)

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* Total Sales (Revenue)
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin (%)
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Total Customers
* Repeat Customers
* Repeat Rate (%)

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 📌 1. Business Overview

* Revenue and profit KPIs
* Sales trend over time
* Seasonal performance insights

### 📌 2. Category Analysis

* Sales vs Profit comparison
* Identification of loss-making sub-categories

### 📌 3. Discount Analysis

* Discount vs Profit relationship
* Impact of high discounting on profitability

### 📌 4. Region Analysis

* Regional sales and profit comparison
* Region-category level performance

### 📌 5. Customer Analysis

* RFM-based customer segmentation
* Customer distribution and revenue contribution
* High-value customer identification

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* High discounts negatively impact profitability, especially in the Furniture category
* Sub-categories like Tables and Bookcases are loss-making
* The West region performs best, while some regions underperform
* A small group of customers contributes a large portion of total revenue
* Strong repeat customer behavior indicates high retention

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategies to reduce losses
* Focus on high-performing categories like Technology
* Improve performance in underperforming regions
* Target high-value customers with personalized strategies
* Implement retention and loyalty programs
